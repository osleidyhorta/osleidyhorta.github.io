~About Me~

My name is Osleidy Horta. I was born in Habana, Cuba on May 10th, 2000. As a newborn in Cuba life was not all that easy for my parents since they were always trying to make it a little easier for me, In the year 2003, my mom won a lottery contest that gave my family and I visas to the United States, since my grandfather was already here, he payed for our expenses and we made it to the land of the free. I quickly started school and I learned English with the unconditional  help of my cousins and family friends. Suddenly another door of opportunity opened when I met Mr.Pate and I had the chance to join the Technology program that at the time was nothing but and elective class.

I quickly fell in love with graphic design and I just wanted to learn more and more than just the basic of photoshop, so the next year in 8th grade I decided to join the magnet. Although graphic designing and technology isn't exactly the road I want to take I do believe that my knowledge on 3d printing and designing in general can help me help my patients in the near future. Fast forward 13 years of hard work and support from my mom, who was just as new as I was in this country. I am where I am right now, trying my best to get scholarships and continue my education. School and education is very important to me, since I will be one of the first people in my family of immigrants who makes it to college.
